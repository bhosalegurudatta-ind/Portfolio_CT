Hey, I'm Gurudatta Bhosale — a first-year B.Tech student with an unrelenting passion for Data Science. I believe data holds the key to solving the world's most complex problems, and I'm here to unlock it.

My journey started with C Programming — learning to think like a machine — before discovering the expressive power of Python and the web through HTML & CSS. SQL opened my eyes to the beauty of structured information.

I've earned certifications in Python Essentials 1 from Cisco Networking Academy and Claude 101 from Anthropic, validating my foundational programming and AI skills. More are on the way!

When I'm not crunching numbers, you'll find me on the cricket pitch, charging down a football field, or deep in strategy on a gaming session.
